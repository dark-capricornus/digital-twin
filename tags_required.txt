Parameters to be showcased
Melting and holding furnace:
Core Energy

Furnace_Instant_kW (Real-time electrical load drawn by the furnace.The actual power being consumed at that exact second.(SCADA reads that kW tag from power meter via a PLC)
Furnace_Total_kWh (cummulative energy-        Energy+=kW×(Δt/3600))

Temperature

Melt_Bath_Temperature (Actual molten aluminium temperature)
Zone_Temperatures (Live temperature readings from multiple furnace zones (roof, wall, bath)

Status

Furnace_Mode (CHARGE/MELT/ HOLD/ IDLE/PREHEAT/TAPPING/CLEANING)
Furnace_Run_Status(RUNNING/ STOPPED/FAULT/MAINTENANCE)
Alarm_Status (safety / fault indicator
Melt/hold timers(Elapsed or remaining time for melt and holding phases.)

LPDC_01, LPDC_02 and LPDC_03:

 Core Energy
LPDC_Instant_kW
Real-time electrical load of LPDC machine (hydraulics, heaters, motors).

LPDC_Total_kWh
Cumulative electrical energy consumed by the LPDC machine.

 Pressure (Core Process Variable)
Riser_Pressure
Actual air pressure applied to push molten metal into die.

Pressure_Setpoint
Target pressure defined in casting recipe.

Holding_Pressure
Pressure maintained during solidification phase.

 Temperature
Holding_Furnace_Temperature
Molten metal temperature feeding the LPDC machine.

Die_Top_Temperature
Temperature of upper die half.

Die_Bottom_Temperature
Temperature of lower die half.

 Time / Cycle
Cycle_Time
Total time taken for one casting cycle.

Fill_Time
Time taken to fill die cavity with molten metal.

Solidification_Time
Time metal is held under pressure before release.

 Status


LPDC_Run_Status
RUNNING / STOPPED / FAULT

Cycle_Status
FILLING / HOLDING / COOLING / EJECTING

Alarm_Status
Safety / fault indicator (e.g., Low Pressure, Die Over-Temp, Hydraulic Fault)

 Production
Shot_Count
Total number of casting cycles completed at time instance.(eg 10 am- 100, 11am- 150, etc)

Recipie/ Model Id
Model number of the die
